Privacy Policy for Habiter

Effective Date: December 19, 2024

Welcome to Habiter ("the Website"). Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our services. By using Habiter, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Note: "Habiter" is the in-house name for a game called "Tome of Heroes."

1. Information We Collect

Habiter interacts with your Google account to access:

  • Google Drive:
    • Create and read JSON files to store and load your custom data for the Website.
  • Google Tasks:
    • Retrieve the number of tasks you’ve recently completed.
    • Analyze task descriptions to infer task types and difficulty levels for gamification purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected from your Google Drive and Google Tasks is used solely to:

  • Store and load your custom data for personalized experiences.
  • Enhance the gamification features by analyzing your task completion activity.

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any other purpose outside of providing the core functionality of Habiter.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Google Drive: JSON files are created and accessed only with your permission. These files are stored in your Google Drive, not on our servers.
  • Security Measures: We use industry-standard encryption protocols to secure the connection between your Google account and our Website.
  • No Data Retention: We do not store any of your data outside of your Google Drive or Google Tasks integration.

4. Third-Party Access

  • We do not sell, share, or otherwise provide your data to third parties.
  • All access to Google APIs complies with Google's policies and is subject to their terms of service.

5. User Control

  • Granting and Revoking Access: You can manage and revoke the Website’s access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account permissions.
  • Data Deletion: Deleting the JSON files from your Google Drive or modifying your Google Tasks is entirely under your control. If you wish to stop using Habiter, no additional steps are needed to ensure your data is removed.

6. Children’s Privacy

Habiter is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, with a revised "Effective Date."
